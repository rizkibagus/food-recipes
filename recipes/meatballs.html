How to Make Cheese-Filled Beef Meatballs

Ingredients
  • 250 grams of beef
  • 100 grams of starch
  • 100 grams of cheddar cheese, cut into cubes
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon of flavoring
  • 1 liter of water to boil
  • 1 garlic clove, puree
  • 1/2 teaspoon of flavoring
  • 1/2 tablespoon chicken oil
  • 1/4 tsp nutmeg
  • 1/4 tsp pepper
  • 1/4 tsp of flavoring
  • 1 liter of beef broth
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 tsp pepper
  • ice cubes as needed
Here The Instructions
  1. Mix all meatball ingredients except cheese and water. After everything is well blended, put it in a food processor or blender until itâ€™s completely smooth
  2. Bring the water to a boil. Print the round meatballs by hand and fill the center with cheese
  3. Put the meatballs in boiling water and cook until the meatballs float (approximately 5 minutes), remove from heat
  4. Heat the broth to a boil in a separate pan, put the cooked meatballs
  5. Add oil, salt, flavorings and pepper.
  6. Meatballs are ready to be served.
